Teacher FAQ - Student Answers

Why is wearing your smartwatch not allowed during school hours?

I understand that watches can be distracting, but today I wore mine responsibly and only checked the time once. It didn’t distract me or others.

How do you use your smartwatch during class?

I keep notifications off and only use it to check the time discreetly. I didn’t play games or use any other apps during class.

Does your smartwatch connect to the school Wi-Fi or network?

No, my watch cannot connect to the guest or main Wi-Fi because it doesn’t support the school’s Wi-Fi types. It only connects via my phone, which is mostly off Wi-Fi during school.

Do other devices with similar features get special permission?

It seems some devices, like other smartwatches with games, are allowed, and it feels unfair that I’m not given the same chance despite being responsible.
